Welcome to Bulletlist.report!

The news aggregation app that allows you to quickly scan through news headlines without distracting images or advertisements.

Here's a quick summary of the main features:

  • Create a user account and customize your available news feeds.
  • After signing in, easily create exportable bookmarks and view bookmarks saved by other users.
  • Press tab or double-click on the page to iterate through each news feed source.
  • Select the # within each feed to minimize/maximize the list from view.
  • Visit the About page to learn more.
